Our Pledge To You

Our corporate clients understand that a company’s most valuable resource is its employees. An employee’s decision to move with a company is a major event which may have a massive impact on both the company, the individual and their family. At Premier Relocation Services we appreciate your need to be sure that the corporate relocation company you choose will deliver a service which, as well as being value for money, will also provide total peace of mind for both the company and the employee. We will support your employees every step of the way and handle all aspects of their relocation.

We also understand that you will require clear costs at the outset, and our assurance that the services agreed are met fully and to your complete satisfaction. We will provide you with regular progress reports, keeping you fully in the picture that everything is being done to ensure a smooth transition and fast assimilation for your valuable key employees and their family. We aim to take the stress and pressure out of a company’s corporate relocation, allowing your employees the time totally to focus on their role. We are able to offer flexible relocation services which can be modified to you and your employee requirements.These include:

Rental Property search

We understand that finding a new home is one of the most important aspects of any relocation. Our rental search service is designed to make the whole process as hassle and as stress free as possible. We always try to fit in at least 6 property viewings per rental search and these properties will be based on your specific needs and requirements.

Orientation tour

Before we begin a rental search we highly recommend an orientation tour of potential locations as it’s a great way for your employee and their family to view different locations and sample housing within their budget so they can get an idea of where they would prefer to live and how far their budget achieves within desired locations. If they have children we can include a couple of visits to suitable schools too.

School search – private and state

Most parents biggest concern is finding the right school for their children. Following an initial discussion suitable nursery/school options would be identified which can be in the state or private sector. Premier Relocation will liaise with local education authorities as well as the preferred schools to check availability of places and support employees with a school search. We will also provide advice and statistics on the performance of the most schools as well as the relevant prospectus with details of how to gain entrance and the relevant admission procedures. We can arrange visits to schools and can accompany your employee to provide support at those visits.

Settling in services

As well as finding a home in the UK there are additional support services, settling in services, which we can offer your employees to help them get settled into their new UK lifestyles as quickly and effortlessly as possible

Serviced Accommodation

If your employee requires somewhere to stay in the short term in the UK, we can help source suitable serviced accommodation located in the area which best works for your employee. These properties are typically serviced and include free wifi and are cleaned regularly just like a hotel. Whilst a hotel may seem like the easier option, serviced apartments offer a number of huge advantages that make them the most comfortable and cost-effective option. Unlike hotel rooms that are often cramped, serviced apartments have plenty of space to move in. Apartments can range from a studio to a 4 bedroom apartment for those with a larger family. We also have pet friendly short-term serviced accommodation.
